Theremin Circuit

The Theremin is a musical device that changes pitch depending on the height of your hand above the instrument. The circuit determines the amount of light being absorbed by a photoresistor, and produces an associated pitch based on that quantitative value. An Arduino Uno was used to convert the voltage across the photoresistor into a frequency, and returned the frequency as a square wave. The square wave traveled through a preamp and power amplifier, then came out as sound through the speaker.


Materials used:

Arduino Uno
2 Photoresistors
Resistors
Voltage Source
OpAmp
Power Amplifier
Speakers
